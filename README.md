# Canadian Food Service Industry Analysis

Small provinces are outgrowing big cities. Atlantic Canada is gaining momentum. Using Statistics Canada's monthly food service revenue data (2018-2025), this project analyzes Canada's food service industry recovery from COVID, seasonal staffing patterns, market structure shifts, and provincial expansion opportunities - built as two complementary deliverables.

## Key Findings

- **National Recovery Index: 131.7** in 2025 - the industry is 31.7% above pre-COVID levels
- **New Brunswick leads YoY growth at +7.01%**, outpacing both Ontario and BC
- Market structure (full-service vs. limited-service) remained **largely unchanged** post-COVID, despite predictions of a permanent shift
- **Ontario is Canada's dominant market at $79.9B**, growing above the national average
- **Atlantic Canada is quietly outgrowing every large market** in 2025
